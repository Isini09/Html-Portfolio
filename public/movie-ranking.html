The Best Movies According to Isini

My top 3 movies of all-time.

3 Idiots

First of all this is an evergreen movie and my favourite movie, there is no lack of comedy, romance and emotional scenes.
This movie is mainly based on life of engineering students more commonlu university students and how they deal with their life when they first come to college.
We can also see a brief overview of hostel Life, how they spend their days at the hostel and how they deal with the constant exam pressure.
We can also learn many life lessons from this movie. Over all the movie is Absolutely Amazing.

Nimona

I can't sing enough praises for this absolute masterfully made film!!
The animation is GORGEOUS and so dynamic and incredible! The artstyle is so unique and each frame is full of immense detail and awe.
The story,-oh my goodness- is beautiful, compelling, heartbreaking and truly poignant, and left me on the verge of tears towards the end.
The overarching message and themes of prejudice, belonging, conformance, and morality were all translated perfectly in this film.

The Govenant

A must watch Guy Ritchie's film and to be remembered.
From the stellar directing to the perfect screenwriting, excellent cinematography, spot-on score, and stellar casting with outstanding performances by everyone.
